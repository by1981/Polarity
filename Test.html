Sensitivity Analysis of News twitter feeds

Every word in a tweet has mood associated with it. The Sensitivity Analysis can be used to analyze the nature of the mood of specific tweet. In this example, we study the moods of the tweets posted by the top five news sources in United States. Each word in a tweet is assiged a polarity value and based on that value an overall tweet sensitivity is calculated.
